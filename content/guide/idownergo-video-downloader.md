## How to Use the iDownerGo Download Function

### How to Download Video from YouTube

**Step 1: Copy the YouTube Video URL**

Navigate to YouTube.com and locate the video you want to download. Copy its URL from the address bar at the top of your browser.

**Step 2: Set the Output Format**

Click the **"Settings"** button in the top right corner of the iDownerGo interface. In the Preferences window, go to the **"Download"** section. Here, you can select whether you want the file as video or audio, pick the quality, and choose where to save it. Videos are saved as MP4 by default.

**Step 3: Paste the Copied URL**

In iDownerGo, you don't need to manually paste the video URL. Simply click the **"Paste URL"** button, and the software will automatically analyze the video.

**Step 4: Download the YouTube Video**

After the analysis is complete, the download will start automatically. The video will be saved to your computer once the download finishes. You can access the downloaded files in the **"Downloaded"** section of the software.

## Understand the Basics

### 1. Preferences

In the Preference Option, you can adjust the following settings:

- **Maximum Download Tasks:** Set the number of simultaneous downloads to boost efficiency.
- **Output Format:** Choose whether you want the output as video or audio.
- **Quality:** Select the video resolution or audio quality.
- **Destination Location:** Change where the downloaded files will be saved.
- **Additional Settings:** Enable options like "Auto Download Subtitles" and "Auto Resume Unfinished Tasks" as needed.
- **Private Videos:** For downloading private videos from Facebook or Instagram, make sure to select "Allow to read Cookies from Firefox/Chrome" before starting.

### 2. Turn on Turbo-fast Mode

Turbo-fast Mode is the default speed for downloads. You can adjust the download speed by selecting a different mode in the lower left corner of the interface or through the Preferences option.

In the Preferences under Connection Options, you can also use an in-app proxy. Check the **"Enable proxy"** box and enter your HTTP proxy details, including port, account, and password.

### 3. Pause or Resume the Downloading Files

In iDownerGo, you can pause a download by clicking the **"Pause"** button. To stop all downloads at once, click the **"Pause All"** button in the top-right corner. To continue downloading, click the **"Resume"** button.

### 4. Protect Your Video Privacy

iDownerGo offers a private video mode to keep your videos and music files secure in a password-protected folder.

- Go to the **"Downloaded"** section and right-click on the video you want to protect.
- Select **"Move to Private List"** from the options.
- Enter your password to secure the video. You can also choose to hide the video and its thumbnail.
- Click the lock icon to complete the process and move the video to the private list.

### 5. Change Interface Language

iDownerGo supports various languages, such as English, Chinese, Italian, and French. To change the language, click the three-line menu button in the top-right corner, select the **"Languages"** tab, and choose your preferred language from the list.
